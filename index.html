How to prepare it

Fufu
Ingredients

4 cups corn flour (500g or half kg)

8 cups water (2 liters)

Instructions

Place 6 and a half cups of water in a pot and bring to a boil.

One cup of the corn flour is placed a bowl.

Add 1 cup of water to the flour and mix to form a smooth paste.

Add another half cup of water and mix well to form a loose creamy mixture (with the consistency of liquid whole milk).

When water boils, carefully subtract 5 cups of the water, placing it in a hot water safe container.

Add in all of the mixture, while stirring continuously so as to avoid forming lumps.

The mixture will thicken up as you stir.

Now the flour and mix well to incorporate.

Once the flour is all mixed in, add half of the reserved hot water to the pot.

Cover and let it cook for 5 minutes on medium heat.

After that, open and mix the fufu with the water in circular motion until well incorporated.

Add the remaining hot water then cover and let it cook for another 5 minutes.

Mix the fufu again with the hot water until well incorporated.

Now let it cook for about 3 minutes and your corn fufu is done!
